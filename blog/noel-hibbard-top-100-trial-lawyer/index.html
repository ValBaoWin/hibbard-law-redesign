Noel Hibbard Named Top 100 Trial Lawyer for Fifth Consecutive Year

We are proud to announce this continued recognition of our Managing Attorney's dedication to exceptional advocacy.

N
Noel Hibbard, Esq.
Managing Attorney

Hibbard Law Group is proud to announce that Managing Attorney Noel Hibbard, Esq. has once again been recognized as a Top 100 Trial Lawyer by the American Trial Lawyers association — marking the fifth consecutive year he has earned this prestigious designation.

About the Recognition

The Top 100 Trial Lawyers designation is awarded by the American Trial Lawyers association to attorneys who demonstrate exceptional skill, dedication, and results in trial advocacy. Selection is based on a rigorous evaluation of professional experience, peer recognition, and demonstrated outcomes for clients.

Fewer than 1% of attorneys in each state receive this recognition each year, making it one of the most selective and meaningful honors in the legal profession.

A Track Record Built Over 22+ Years

Noel Hibbard began his career on the defense side, representing insurance companies and government entities — giving him an insider's understanding of how the other side evaluates and defends claims. In 2011, he founded Hibbard Law Group to dedicate his practice entirely to representing injured workers and accident victims in California.

Since then, the firm has recovered over $5 million for clients across personal injury and workers' compensation matters, handling cases from prelitigation settlement through trial.

"Being recognized five years in a row is humbling, but what matters most to me is the outcomes we achieve for our clients. Every recognition reflects their trust in us to fight for what they deserve."

Prior Recognitions

In addition to the Top 100 Trial Lawyers designation, Noel has previously been named:

  • Top 10 Attorney — American Institute of Personal Injury Attorneys (2019)
  • Top 100 Attorney — American Trial Lawyers (2019)

He is a member of the California State Bar, the California Applicant Attorneys' Association, and the Santa Clara County Bar Association, and regularly lectures to chiropractors on legal ethics and personal injury law.
