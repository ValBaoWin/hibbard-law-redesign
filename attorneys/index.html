Noel Hibbard, Esq.

Career & Background

Noel Hibbard began his legal career as an insurance defense attorney at Cholakian & Associates, representing insurance companies, government entities, and their insureds in personal injury matters. This insider perspective gave him an invaluable understanding of how insurers evaluate and defend claims — knowledge he now uses entirely in his clients' favor.

He later worked at Bennett, Samuelsen, Reynolds & Allard, defending insureds in catastrophic personal injury matters including auto accidents, slip-and-fall incidents, and homeowner claims. He also served as a court-appointed conflict attorney for Alameda County, deepening his courtroom experience across a wide range of case types.

In 2011, Noel opened his own practice with a clear mission: to use everything he learned on the defense side to fight for injured Californians. His firm specializes in serious personal injury and workers' compensation matters, including traumatic brain injuries (TBI) and wrongful death claims — exactly the types of cases where experience and tenacity matter most.

Distinctions & Leadership

Noel is regularly recognized by his peers for excellence in trial advocacy. He was named a Top 10 Attorney by the American Institute of Personal Injury Attorneys (2019) and a Top 100 Attorney by the American Trial Lawyers (2019), and has earned the Top 100 Trial Lawyer designation for five consecutive years.

Beyond courtroom advocacy, Noel regularly lectures to chiropractors on legal ethics and personal injury practice — a reflection of his commitment to elevating standards across the legal and medical community.

Alex Halkias, Esq.

"Ready To Fight For What You Deserve."
Career & Background

Alex Halkias brings over a decade of focused experience in workers' compensation law, dedicating his entire legal career to advocating for injured workers across the Bay Area. He earned his Juris Doctor from Santa Clara University School of Law, one of the nation's leading law schools for Bay Area practice.

From the beginning, Alex focused exclusively on workers' compensation — developing a deep, nuanced understanding of the challenges employees face when navigating the often-complex claims process. His singular focus means he knows the system inside and out, anticipating insurer tactics and advocating forcefully for the full range of benefits his clients are entitled to receive.

Approach & Philosophy

Alex is known among colleagues and clients alike for meticulous case preparation and unwavering client service. He believes that thorough preparation — understanding every detail of a client's medical history, work situation, and the applicable law — is the foundation of successful advocacy.

His approach has resulted in numerous successful settlements and verdicts for injured workers who might otherwise have been denied the benefits they deserved. Outside of work, Alex enjoys hiking, running, and spending time with his partner and young daughter — a reminder of what truly matters and why the work he does every day is worth doing.
