About the Firm

Over Two Decades of
Winning for the Injured

Hibbard Law Group has been practicing law for over 22 years in Personal Injury and Workers' Compensation, and has won millions of dollars in settlements for clients. No matter how difficult or complex your case may seem, we will do everything in our power to get you the justice and compensation you deserve.

Each of our lawyers brings a unique background and perspective to serious personal injury, wrongful death, elder abuse, and auto accident cases. We have extensive experience and success at every stage of litigation — from prelitigation settlement to litigation and trial.
